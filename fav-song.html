    Why is Sauti sol band my favourite

    1. Sauti Sol you've been following Kenyan music for the past few years - or 10 - then you know that Sauti Sol is also a big name.So whenever they announce a new song, everyone goes nuts.
    2. Uthentically Kenyan Taking Extravaganza is laced with Sheng and Swahili lines that only Kenyans can understand and relate to.
    3. Rhumba Sound Talk about nostalgia. Rhumba imetamba and it will live forever thanks to Extravaganza. This classic Kenyan song will most likely feature in our zilizopendwa playlist when we’re 50.
    4. The OutfitTwo words, Just. Wow. Sauti Sol and Sol Generation brought back kaunda suits in the funkiest colours ever. Styled by Brian Babu, the Kenyan kings made them 2019sque by opening the buttons (hey Nviiri) and accessorizing with shades and hats.
    5. Sol Generation We weren’t expecting it. But this East African hit was the perfect intro to the talented kids under their experienced wings. After all, Sol Generation Records is Sauti Sol’s way of ushering the next generation of soul artists in Kenya.
